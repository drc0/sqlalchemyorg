SQLAlchemy 1.4.16 is now available.

With the pace of regressions having slowed, it is hoped that the 1.4
series continues to achieve wide adoption, which will further establish the
stability of the new architectures and features.    Release 1.4.16 includes
a variety of fixes in including some minor regressions in the ORM.

Thanks to our users and developers for their continued support and efforts
on the project.

The complete changelog for 1.4.16 is at `Changelog </changelog/CHANGES_1_4_16>`_.

SQLAlchemy 1.4.16 is available on the `Download Page </download.html>`_.
